    What is Airbnb?

    How does providing identification on Airbnb work?

    Verifying information about guests and hosts helps make our community safer for everyone. In addition to requiring a profile photo, phone number, and email address, we may also ask for an official ID and other information.

    When am I charged for a reservation?
